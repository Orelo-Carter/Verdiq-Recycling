About Us

Verdiq Recycling Limited is a purpose-driven recycling company advancing sustainable waste management and climate action in Nigeria. Headquartered in Ibadan, with operational locations in Abeokuta (Ogun State) and Ado-Ekiti (Ekiti State), we serve as a regional recycling hub connecting waste generators to responsible recycling markets.

We focus on recovering PET plastics and white paper waste, materials that are commonly mismanaged and contribute significantly to environmental pollution and carbon emissions when burned or dumped.

What Makes Us Different?

  1. Community-first model: Door-to-door sensitization and engagement.
  2. Education-led recycling: School-to-school awareness programs for pupils, students, and teachers.
  3. Carbon data tracking: Measuring CO₂ emissions avoided through waste diversion.
  4. Circular economy focus: Turning waste into economic and environmental value.

Our work goes beyond collection; we build awareness, behavior change, and data-backed climate solutions that can scale across Nigeria and Africa.

Our Services

Waste Collection & Aggregation

We collect PET plastics (blue & white) and white paper waste from:

  • Households.
  • Offices and institutions.
  • Schools.
  • Community collection points.

Recovered waste is sorted, measured, baled, and supplied to verified recycling off-takers.

School Recycling & Environmental Education

We partner with primary and secondary schools to:

  • Educate schools on recycling.
  • Implement waste separation systems.
  • Enable sale of recyclables to Verdiq.
  • Support Green Clubs for sustainability.

Carbon Emissions Tracking & Reporting

We track avoided carbon emissions from diverted waste to support:

  • Climate impact reporting.
  • Sustainability partnerships.
  • ESG and carbon reduction initiatives.

Door-to-Door Awareness & Community Engagement

Our teams engage communities directly to:

  • Promote responsible waste disposal.
  • Door-to-Door Waste pick-up.
  • Reduce open dumping and burning of waste.
  • Increase participation in recycling programs.

Recycling Data & Sustainability Solutions

Through ongoing technology development, Verdiq is building systems to:

  • Digitally track waste volumes and sources.
  • Support transparent reporting.
  • Enable data-driven climate and sustainability decision-making.

Sustainable Consulting & ESG Advisory

Data-Driven Sustainability Solutions for Businesses and Institutions.

Verdiq Recycling Limited provides Sustainable Consulting services that help organizations measure, manage, and improve their environmental performance through calculation waste based (CO₂) averted by selling the waste and not burning/dumping in landfills.
